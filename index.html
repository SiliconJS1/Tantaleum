What is Tantalum ?

Tantalum is a rare, hard metal used in the electronics industry for capacitors, high power resistors, and other electronic components. It is heavily used by semiconductors companies to build mobile phones, tablets, computers, TV screens, automotive electronics, satellites, and any other electronic equipments.

World wide market

Tantalum is used to build electronics all over the world, phones, tablets, computers, TV screens, automotives, satellites, ...

Increasing demand

Tantalum market price is expected to skyrocket in the near future due to the growing demand and development for new technologies such as smart devices, electric and hybrid cars, and self driving vehicles.

Market share

Tantalum production reached 1200 tons/year and is on target to reach a 40% annual growth rate.

Project

Tantaleum is a new cryptocurrency token created to leverage the world market of Tantalum mineral trading. Our initial goal is to transcribe the global world market of Tantalum ore with a decentralized technology in order to virtually track and follow its growth. By burning tokens over time, Tantaleum strives to soar in the same degree and volatility as the mineral prices, ideal for long term investment as a safe-haven currency.

Based on the outcome of the initial token sale, we then aim to build an official crypto currency market allowing industries to quickly and efficiently buy from miners and electronics recycling factories accross the world, at a fair price. By connecting suppliers, manufacturers, and intermediaries using a decentralized network, Tantaleum aims to provide a sustainable market respectful of small suppliers of Tantalum mineral.
